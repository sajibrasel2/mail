## How to Use

### Step 1: Go to Compose Page
Navigate to the Compose section of your dashboard.

### Step 2: Select Template Mode
Choose "Use Professional Template" (pre-selected by default).

### Step 3: Choose Category
Select the appropriate template based on your target audience:
- **Developer (GitHub)** - For technical professionals
- **Web (General)** - For general business professionals
- **Gaming** - For gaming community members
- **Marketing** - For marketing professionals
- **Business** - For executives and founders
- **Freelancer** - For freelance workers
- **Blogger** - For content creators
- **Job Seeker** - For people seeking employment

### Step 4: Enter Your Link
Paste your website URL, offer link, or affiliate link. This is inserted as the main CTA.

### Step 5: (Optional) Customize Sender
Expand "Customize Sender Details" to personalize:
- Your name
- Your email
- Your phone number
- Your title (for Business template)

### Step 6: Preview
Click "Preview Template" to see how the email will look with your information.

### Step 7: Send
Click "Send to Pending Leads" to send personalized emails to all recipients in the selected category.

## Best Practices

1. **Use the Right Category**
   - Select the category that best matches your target audience
   - Wrong category = lower engagement

2. **Compelling Links**
   - Use tracking URLs to monitor clicks: `https://yoursite.com/offer?utm_campaign=marketing`
   - Include special offers or unique value propositions

3. **Sender Details Matter**
   - Always include your name and email
   - Phone number increases trust and response rates
   - Professional title adds credibility (especially for Business template)

4. **Test First**
   - Always preview before sending
   - Check that all placeholders are replaced correctly
   - Verify your link works

5. **Follow-up**
   - Plan follow-up emails for non-responders
   - Track metrics: open rates, click rates, conversions
   - A/B test different links to find what works
