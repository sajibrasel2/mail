HOW TO USE:
===========

1. Go to http://127.0.0.1:5000/compose

2. Select mode: "Use Professional Template" (default)

3. Choose category:
   - Developer (GitHub) - for tech professionals
   - Web (General) - for business professionals
   - Gaming - for gamers/streamers
   - Marketing - for marketing professionals
   - Business - for executives/founders
   - Freelancer - for freelancers
   - Blogger - for content creators
   - Job Seeker - for job seekers

4. Enter your link:
   - https://yoursite.com/offer
   - https://yoursite.com/webinar
   - https://yoursite.com/partnership
   - Any URL

5. (Optional) Customize sender:
   - Your name
   - Your email
   - Your phone
   - Your title

6. Click "Preview Template" to see the email

7. Click "Send to Pending Leads"

8. Done! All recipients in that category get personalized emails.
